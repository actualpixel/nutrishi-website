Privacy Policy

Nutrishi is offline-first by design. Your data stays on this device unless you choose otherwise.

Your profile, food logs, water logs, weight records, goals, reminders, and preferences are stored locally on your device at this stage.

Detailed policy
⌾

Information stored on your device

  • Profile details
  • Calorie and macro targets
  • Food logs and saved foods
  • Meal templates
  • Water logs and water goals
  • Weight records
  • Reminder and appearance preferences
⌁

How the app uses this information

  • Estimate calorie needs
  • Calculate macro targets
  • Track daily nutrition
  • Track hydration and weight history
  • Personalize local suggestions
  • Schedule local reminders if enabled
i

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health or nutrition data with advertisers or third parties. At this stage, the app does not send your profile, food logs, water logs, or weight records to our servers.

◷

Notifications and reminders

Reminders in this app are local check-ins. Notification permission may be requested so meal, water, weight, and weekly progress reminders can appear on your device. You can disable reminders from app settings or from your device settings at any time.

≡

Data control

You can edit your profile information inside the app and, where supported, update logged entries from existing tracking flows. You can also reset local data from Profile → Data & Privacy to remove locally stored information from this device.

⚙

App Lock

App Lock uses your device authentication to help protect access to Nutrishi. Nutrishi does not store your Face ID, fingerprint, or biometric data. Biometric authentication is handled by your device operating system.

i

Health disclaimer

This app is for general wellness and nutrition tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Calorie, macro, water, and BMI values are estimates. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major diet, fitness, pregnancy, medical, or weight-loss decisions.

Offline-first storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, clear app data, or reset local data, this information may be deleted from the device.

Export and import

Nutrishi may allow you to export and import local backup files. These files may contain personal nutrition and health-related information. Keep backup files private and store them in a secure location. Nutrishi does not upload these backup files unless you choose to share them using your device’s share options.

Children

This app is intended for personal wellness tracking. It is not designed for children without parent or guardian supervision.
