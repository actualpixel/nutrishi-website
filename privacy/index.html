Privacy Policy

Nutrishi is offline-first by design. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

Your profile, food logs, water logs, weight records, goals, reminders, preferences, and local backups are stored locally on your device in the current version.

Quick privacy highlights
⌾

Stored locally

Nutrition, water, weight, and profile data stays on your device.

i

We do not use advertising trackers in the current version.

≡

No selling

We do not sell your health or nutrition data.

Detailed policy
⌾

Information stored on your device

  • Profile details such as name, gender, age/date of birth, height, and weight
  • Calorie, macro, water, and weight goals
  • Food logs, saved foods, custom foods, and meal templates
  • Meal plans and planned food items
  • Water logs and hydration records
  • Weight records and progress history
  • Reminder, appearance, app lock, and other app preferences
⌁

How the app uses this information

  • Estimate calorie needs and daily nutrition targets
  • Calculate macro targets and progress summaries
  • Track daily nutrition, hydration, meal plans, and weight history
  • Show local insights based on your own app data
  • Schedule local reminders if enabled
  • Protect access with App Lock if enabled
i

Data collection and sharing

In the current version, Nutrishi does not require an account and does not send your profile, food logs, water logs, meal plans, weight records, or goals to our servers.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health or nutrition data with advertisers or third parties.

⇄

Export and import backups

Nutrishi may allow you to export and import local backup files. These files can contain personal nutrition and health-related information.

Keep backup files private and store them securely. Nutrishi does not upload backup files unless you choose to share them using your device’s share options.

◷

Notifications and reminders

Reminders are local check-ins. Notification permission may be requested so meal, water, weight, and weekly progress reminders can appear on your device. You can disable reminders from app settings or from your device settings at any time.

≡

Data control and deletion

You can edit profile details and logged entries inside the app where supported. You can also reset local data from Profile → Data & Privacy to remove locally stored Nutrishi information from this device.

If you uninstall the app, clear app data, change devices without importing a backup, or reset local data, your local Nutrishi data may be deleted from the device.

⚙

App Lock

App Lock uses your device authentication to help protect access to Nutrishi. Nutrishi does not store your Face ID, fingerprint, or biometric data. Biometric authentication is handled by your device operating system.

i

Health disclaimer

Nutrishi is for general wellness and nutrition tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Calorie, macro, water, and BMI values are estimates. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major diet, fitness, pregnancy, medical, or weight-loss decisions.

Children

Nutrishi is intended for adults and is not designed for children without parent or guardian supervision.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Nutrishi evolves. If future versions add account login, cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, ads, subscriptions, or server-side features, this policy and relevant store declarations should be updated before release.
