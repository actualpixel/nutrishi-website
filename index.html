Local only

Your data stays on your device.

Nutrishi is designed as an offline-first app. Your profile, logs, goals, preferences, and history are stored locally unless you choose to export or share them.

  • No account required
  • No advertising trackers
  • No selling of health data
  • Local export and import backups
  • Device authentication support through App Lock

Health disclaimer

Nutrishi is for general wellness and nutrition tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Calorie, macro, water, and BMI values are estimates. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major diet, fitness, pregnancy, medical, or weight-loss decisions.
